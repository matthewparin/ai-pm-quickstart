# AGENTS.md

## Who I am
Senior Product Director at Salesforce leading a team of Senior 
PMs and Directors. Focused on ISV/OEM partner products.
Goals: prototype products, evaluate team output, understand 
engineering decisions. Ship small, iterative changes quickly.

## How I work
- Structured, direct output — no fluff
- When reviewing PM work, assess: clarity of problem statement,
  quality of success metrics, and feasibility of the approach
- When writing PRDs: Problem, Users, Goals, Non-goals,
  Requirements, Open Questions
- Flag uncertainty rather than guessing
- Use Plan Mode before making file changes

## Standards I hold my team to
- Quality and clarity of writing
- Quality of engineering choices
- Balancing speed, quality, and scale — in this order
- Every feature requires a clear success metric before scoping
- PRDs must reference user evidence, not assumptions
